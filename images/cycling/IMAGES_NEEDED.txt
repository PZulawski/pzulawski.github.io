LACTATE TESTING PT 1
Page: https://sites.google.com/view/pzulawski/cycling/lactate-testing-pt-1

  lactate-pt1-ramp-test.png
    -> First graph: power output, heart rate, and breath frequency across the full
       ramp test. Black dashed lines mark LT1 at 295W and LT2 at 385W.

  lactate-pt1-breath-freq.png
    -> Second graph: breath frequency across the full protocol, showing three
       distinct regions (ramp-up, plateau, sharp increase at LT2).

  lactate-pt1-breath-vol.png
    -> Third graph: breath frequency alongside total air volume (red line, right
       axis) — volume stays roughly constant through the 295W plateau.

  lactate-pt1-co2-ratio.png
    -> Fourth graph: breath frequency alongside CO2-to-O2 concentration ratio —
       steep gradient visible in the 1500-1800s window marking the LT1 region.

BIKE MODS 2023/2024
Page: https://sites.google.com/view/pzulawski/cycling/bike-mods-20232024

  bike-mods-original-bracket.png
    -> Original Cervelo P5 front-end bracket, shown from the underside — curved
       mating surfaces at top, armrest flat on the opposite side, 'binocular'
       extension clamps at the bottom.

  bike-mods-3d-scan.png
    -> 3D laser scan of the original bracket — point cloud processed into a mesh
       for use in CAD.

  bike-mods-concept-sketch.png
    -> Initial concept sketch for the new bracket — essentially a shelf with the
       mating surfaces replicated and a flat armrest surface appended forward via
       a descending connecting section.

  bike-mods-cad-model.png
    -> Finished CAD model of the new bracket, after several iterations checking
       tolerances, deflection, stiffening rib, and bolt holes.

  bike-mods-machined-part.gif
    -> The finished machined aluminium bracket — carved from a solid block (animated GIF).

  bike-mods-installed-1.png
    -> Bracket installed on the base bar (photo 1) — showing the 0.75mm rubber shim
       and the tight clearance between the descending section and the base bar.

  bike-mods-installed-2.png
    -> Bracket installed on the base bar (photo 2) — additional angle of the
       installed bracket.

  bike-mods-race.png
    -> Bracket in use at the East Anglian VTTA F2a/10, 5th June.
